# Why Best Controllability?

FIBO Studio demonstrates precise output control through a 3D-to-photo pipeline where users visually control every parameter:

- **Camera Control**: 9 preset angles (Front, Back, Left, Right, Top, Bottom, Hero) + fine-tune X/Y/Z sliders with real-time PiP preview
- **Lighting Control**: 4 mood presets + manual key light intensity, color, and ambient controls
- **Color Palette**: Direct background and floor color selection via hex picker

Every user interaction maps directly to FIBO's JSON-native parameters (camera angle, shot_type, lighting type, color_palette). Users don't write prompts hoping for the right angle - they position a camera, see a live preview, and get exactly what they composed. This is FIBO's controllability made visual and intuitive.
